Vogelwarte NIMBLE workshop. Overview of examples.

April 2018

Perry de Valpine, UC Berkeley

Examples list

The examples we may use include:

  • pump failure times, a WinBUGS classic.
  • state-space population model: house martins
  • simple capture-recapture (CJS): dippers
  • capture-recapture from a transect the Czech republic.
  • simulated N-mixture model with a couple of covariates
  • zero-inflated Poisson N-mixture model: Swiss Great Tits
  • multi-state capture-recapture: ladyslipper orchids
  • simulated occupancy model with a couple of covariates
  • integrated population model: woodchat shrikes
